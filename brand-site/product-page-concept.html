About this fixture

A personalizable, cylindrical wall sconce with recessed sockets — perfect for tucking bulbs out of your line of sight while providing comfortable general or accent light. The clean geometry works in bathrooms, hallways, bedrooms, and anywhere you want a modern but warm wall light.

  • Available in all 13 Dutton Brown signature colors
  • Three metal finish options: brass, nickel, or black
  • Hardwired installation with included mounting hardware
  • ADA compliant with under 5" projection from wall
  • Pairs well with the Color Ruler Pendant and Globe Flush Mount
