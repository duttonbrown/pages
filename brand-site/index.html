Our Story

Started with one light on Etsy. Now 200+ and counting.

Dutton Brown began in 2013 with a simple idea: lighting should never be boring. Founded by designer Zach Dutton and engineer Thomas Brown, we started as an Etsy shop offering one handcrafted light fixture.

Today, we're a Minneapolis design studio producing over 200 customizable lighting and hardware products — each made-to-order, color-forward, and designed to make a statement.
