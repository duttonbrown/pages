Our Story

Color-forward, made-to-order lighting and hardware. Designed and assembled in Minneapolis.

We believe every space deserves character. Since 2013, we have been making bold, colorful lighting and hardware for people who refuse to settle for ordinary.

How It Started

A designer and an engineer walked into a hardware store

In 2013, Dutton Brown began with a simple frustration: why does every piece of lighting and hardware look the same? The answer was always cost. Color means complexity. Complexity means expense. We decided that was a problem worth solving.

Working from a small studio in Minneapolis, we developed a made-to-order process that puts color at the center of everything. No warehouses of pre-made inventory. No compromise. Just the color you want, built when you order it.

What started as a two-person operation has grown into a team of makers, designers, and color obsessives. The mission has not changed: bring real color to the spaces where people live and work.

Our Process

Made to order means made for you

Every piece begins as raw material and ends as something uniquely yours. Here is how we do it.

1

Design

You choose the fixture and the color. Our team confirms specs, dimensions, and finish details.

2

Color

Your chosen finish is mixed and applied by hand. Thirteen signature colors, or custom-match to any sample.

3

Assembly

Each piece is assembled, wired, and inspected by hand in our Minneapolis studio. No shortcuts.

4

Ship

Carefully packed and shipped direct. Most orders deliver within three to four weeks from order date.

What We Believe

Built on four principles

Quality Craftsmanship

Every joint, every wire, every finish is done by hand. We build things that last because we believe lighting should outlive trends.

Color Customization

Thirteen signature colors plus custom color-matching. We exist so you never have to settle for builder-grade finishes.

Made in Minneapolis

Our studio, our team, our supply chain. Keeping production local means faster turnaround, lower waste, and real accountability.

Sustainability

Made-to-order means zero overstock. We use low-VOC finishes, recyclable packaging, and source materials as locally as possible.
