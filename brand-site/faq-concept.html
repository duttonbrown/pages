Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering, shipping, returns, and more.

Ordering

You can place an order directly through our website by browsing our lighting or hardware collections. Select your product, choose your color, and add it to your cart. Every piece is made to order in your chosen color from our palette of 13 signature finishes. For large or commercial projects, please contact us directly for a personalized quote.
Because every piece is made to order, our standard lead time is approximately 3 to 4 weeks from the date your order is placed. Lead times may vary during peak seasons or for larger orders. We will provide a more specific timeline when your order is confirmed. Rush options may be available upon request.
Yes, color samples are available so you can see our finishes in person before committing. Sample chips are available for purchase on our website. Trade program members receive complimentary samples. Visit our trade program page for more details on trade benefits.
Many of our fixtures can be customized in terms of dimensions, rod or cord length, and configuration. Please reach out to us at hello@duttonbrown.com with your specifications, and our team will work with you on a custom solution. Additional charges and extended lead times may apply to custom sizing.
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Shop Pay. For trade and commercial orders, we can also accommodate purchase orders and wire transfers. Contact us for details.

Shipping & Delivery

We ship to all 50 U.S. states. We also ship to Canada and select international destinations. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. For international orders, the customer is responsible for any applicable duties, taxes, or import fees. Please contact us for an international shipping quote.
Once your order has been completed and ships from our Minneapolis studio, domestic delivery typically takes 3 to 7 business days depending on your location. You will receive a tracking number via email as soon as your order ships. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout for an additional charge.
Yes, we ship to Canada and other international destinations on a case-by-case basis. International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes, which are the responsibility of the buyer. Please contact us before placing an international order so we can provide accurate shipping costs and timelines.
We take great care in packaging every order. Each fixture is individually wrapped and secured with custom foam inserts inside a sturdy corrugated box. Hardware items are packaged with protective materials to prevent scratching during transit. If you receive an item that was damaged in shipping, please contact us immediately with photos and we will make it right.

Returns & Exchanges

Because all of our products are made to order in your chosen color, we generally do not accept returns. However, if there is a defect in materials or workmanship, we will repair or replace the item at no cost. We want you to be completely happy with your purchase, so please reach out to us at hello@duttonbrown.com if you have any concerns.
If you believe your item is eligible for a return due to a manufacturing defect, please email us at hello@duttonbrown.com within 14 days of receiving your order. Include your order number and photos of the issue. Our team will review your case and provide instructions within 1 to 2 business days.
Exchanges are handled on a case-by-case basis. Since each piece is made to order, we may be able to create a new piece in a different color or size for you. A new lead time and potential restocking or remake fee may apply. Please contact us to discuss your options.
We are sorry to hear that. If your item arrives damaged during shipping, please contact us at hello@duttonbrown.com within 48 hours of delivery. Include your order number and clear photos of the damage and the packaging. We will work quickly to send you a replacement at no additional cost.

Trade Program

Our trade program is open to interior designers, architects, builders, contractors, hospitality and commercial specifiers, and design firms. We also welcome developers and property managers working on multi-unit projects. Applicants must provide proof of their professional status, such as a business license, resale certificate, or professional portfolio.
Trade members receive exclusive net pricing, which represents a significant discount off our standard retail prices. You will also have access to CAD and Revit files, spec sheets, complimentary samples, and a dedicated account representative. Volume pricing is available for larger projects. Visit our trade program page for full details.
You can apply for our trade program directly on our trade program page. Fill out the application form with your business details and credentials. Our team reviews applications within 1 to 2 business days, and you will receive a confirmation email once approved with your account details and pricing access.
Yes. Approved trade members receive a complimentary sample kit featuring our 13 signature color finishes. Additional samples for specific projects can be requested at no charge. This allows you and your clients to see and feel the quality of our finishes before specifying.

Custom Orders

While our 13 signature colors are the foundation of everything we make, we can create custom color matches for qualifying projects. Custom colors require a minimum order quantity and additional lead time. Please contact us with your Pantone, RAL, or Benjamin Moore color reference, and we will provide a quote and timeline.
Beyond custom colors, we offer modifications to fixture dimensions, cord or rod lengths, mounting configurations, and hardware finish pairings. For commercial and hospitality projects, we can also work on bespoke designs that are tailored to your space. Reach out to discuss what is possible for your project.
Custom pricing depends on the scope of the modification. Simple adjustments like cord length changes may incur a small upcharge, while fully bespoke designs are quoted individually. We will provide a detailed quote before any work begins so there are no surprises. Volume discounts may apply for larger quantities.
Custom orders typically require 4 to 8 weeks depending on the complexity of the modification and current production schedule. Custom color matches may add an additional 1 to 2 weeks for color development and approval. We will confirm the expected timeline when we provide your custom quote.
Simple modifications like cord length or rod adjustments have no minimum. Custom color matches typically require a minimum of 6 units. Fully bespoke designs are evaluated on a per-project basis. Please contact us with your project details so we can advise on minimums and pricing.

Care & Maintenance

We recommend gently dusting your fixture with a soft, dry cloth. For more thorough cleaning, use a slightly damp cloth with mild soap and water. Avoid abrasive cleaners, scouring pads, or chemical solvents, as these can damage the finish. Always ensure the fixture is turned off and cool before cleaning.
Yes. Our powder coat finishes are exceptionally durable and resistant to chipping, scratching, and fading under normal use. Powder coating is more resilient than traditional liquid paint and provides a consistent, smooth finish. Our finishes are designed to maintain their color and quality for years of everyday use.
While it is technically possible to repaint a powder-coated fixture, we do not recommend it, as it may void the warranty and compromise the finish quality. If you would like a different color, we would be happy to create a new piece in your preferred color from our palette of 13 signature finishes. Contact us for a quote.
Yes. We stock replacement parts for most of our fixtures, including canopies, mounting hardware, cords, and shades. If you need a replacement part, email us at hello@duttonbrown.com with your order number or product name, and we will send you what you need. Most replacement parts ship within 3 to 5 business days.
