Behind the Brand

It started with one light and zero boring ideas.

From a single Etsy listing in 2013 to 200+ products made in Minneapolis — here's how we got here.

[Zach & Thomas — Founders Photo — alt tag required]

A designer and an engineer walk into a workshop...

Zach Dutton, a former West Elm designer, had a vision for lighting that wasn't afraid of color. Thomas Brown, an engineer with a love for precision, knew how to build it right. Together, they created something the lighting world was missing: expressive, customizable fixtures that feel high-end and fun at the same time.

Every piece is still made to order. Still assembled in our Minneapolis studio. Still built by real people who inspect, test, and take pride in the work. The only thing that's changed? We have 200+ products now — and 13 signature colors that work across all of them.

We believe lighting should never be boring. And if you're reading this, you probably do too.

2013 Launched on Etsy with one fixture
2017 Expanded to Minneapolis studio
2020 Introduced hardware line
2022 Launched 13-color integrated system
