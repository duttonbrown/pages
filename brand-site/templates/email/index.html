Email Guidelines Quick Reference

  • Subject lines: Clear, attention-grabbing, informative
  • Preheader: Additive to subject — answer the "why"
  • Body: Inform, engage, entertain. Use brand voice.
  • CTAs: Always lead with a verb (ADA compliance)
  • Buttons: 2-4 words max, verb-first
  • Images: All images must have alt tags (ADA)
  • Visual: Clean with pops of color, bold headlines
  • Flow: Sections should sync, not feel jarring
  • Cadence: 1x/week; savings events get 3 sends
  • Approval: Content Marketing Manager signs off
