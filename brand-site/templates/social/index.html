Social Media Quick Reference

1

Tone: Confident, honest, fun, informative, cheeky. Write like a smart design friend, not a corporation.

2

Caption length: Not short and sweet — we want every post to teach something new. Give substance.

3

Emojis: Welcomed, but use sparingly. Typically 1-4 per post, only when it makes sense.

4

Cadence: IG/FB 1-2x/week. LinkedIn with blogs. Pinterest ads refreshed quarterly. TikTok daily.

5

DMs/Comments: Direct product questions to support@duttonbrown.com. Respond within 24 hours.

6

Approval: Content planned 4 weeks ahead. Reviewed by Content Marketing Manager before posting.
