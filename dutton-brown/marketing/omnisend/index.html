Campaign / Automation Orders Revenue AOV
No Omnisend-attributed orders in this window.
Assumptions
  • Window: rolling 30 days, refreshed daily at ~6am CT
  • Revenue basis: Shopify subtotal_price (pre-tax, pre-shipping — closest match to Omnisend's own math)
  • Attribution: Shopify orders where landing_site carries utm_source=Omnisend. Omnisend embeds the campaign or automation ID in the UTM; we group revenue by that ID
  • Attribution is a floor — Omnisend's own dashboard uses a 5-day click window and will typically report 20–40% higher for the same campaign. This view is deliberately conservative
  • Cancelled orders excluded from revenue totals
  • Email Revenue = Campaign Revenue + Automation Revenue
  • Automation Revenue is flow-level (all messages in a flow combined) — per-message breakdown requires Omnisend Statistics API beta
  • Open Rate / CTR averaged across the last 5 sent campaigns inside the window, weighted by delivered count. Unique opens/clicks are not exposed by the Omnisend API — these are totals
  • Rev / Recipient = Email Revenue ÷ total sends across campaigns in the window. A rough efficiency metric; ignores automation sends
