Assumptions
  • Windows: Last 30D and MTD are rolling; Last Month, YTD, and Last Year are calendar-aligned. Last Month and Last Year are frozen once the period closes — numbers stay stable for exec reports
  • Revenue basis: Shopify subtotal_price (pre-tax, pre-shipping — closest match to Omnisend's own math)
  • Attribution: Shopify orders where landing_site carries utm_source=Omnisend. Omnisend embeds the campaign or automation ID in the UTM; we group revenue by that ID
  • Attribution is a floor — Omnisend's own dashboard uses a 5-day click window and will typically report 20–40% higher for the same campaign. This view is deliberately conservative
  • Cancelled orders excluded from revenue totals
  • Email Revenue = Campaign Revenue + Automation Revenue
  • Automation Revenue is flow-level (all messages in a flow combined) — per-message breakdown requires Omnisend Statistics API beta
  • Open Rate / CTR are delivery-weighted averages across every sent campaign in the window. Unique opens/clicks are not exposed by Omnisend's API — these are totals
  • Leaderboard Open / CTR show per-campaign rates. Automations show — because Omnisend's v5 /automations endpoint doesn't expose open/click counters; the Statistics API beta would fill this in
  • Older windows (Last Year, early YTD) may show smaller numbers than reality because Omnisend UTM tagging wasn't always consistent before 2025
  • KPI vocabulary follows db-operations/docs/marketing-kpi-prep-q2-2026.md
