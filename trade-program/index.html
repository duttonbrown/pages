The models, side by side

Flat vs. Tiered — what the trade-off actually is.

Flat model (V1)

One tier · 20% off
  • +Simplest to build, simplest to explain
  • +No billing system, no renewals, no churn
  • +Fastest to Phase 1 revenue
  • —Undifferentiated — every premium brand runs this
  • —No recurring revenue from the program itself
  • —Lower commitment signal from designers

Tiered model (V2 / V3)

Trade + Power Designer
  • +Paid tier filters higher-intent applicants
  • +Recurring revenue: $200 × N Power Designers per year
  • +Physical kit + concierge = memorable, distinctive
  • +No peer brand runs a paid trade tier — press-worthy
  • —More operational commitments (priority queue, kit fulfillment, 2-hr SLA)
  • —Billing system + renewal workflows needed
  • —Existing wholesale accounts need careful handling at rollout
