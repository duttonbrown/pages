Questions We Can Already Answer
Answered — API Rate Limits

100 requests per 10 seconds (10/sec), 1 concurrent request at a time. Returns 429 if exceeded.

At 500 orders/month (~17/day), normal MO creation is fine. Bulk operations (initial parts import, batch queries for pick lists) will need throttle-aware scripting. This is a known constraint, not a blocker.

Answered — Sandbox / Test Environment

No dedicated sandbox. The trial connects to your live Shopify store, but with a key safeguard: only new paid orders created after setup are imported — existing/historical orders are not pulled in.

Recommended approach: Connect a Shopify development store (with generated test data) during the trial to avoid any risk to real orders. Shopify dev stores are free and come with test order generators.

Answered — Data Export / Ownership

Yes, you can export everything. CSV export available from every module (items, BOMs, MOs, POs, inventory). Full database backup at Settings → Database Maintenance → Create a Backup. No vendor lock-in on data.

Answered — Trial Duration

15 + 15 days (30 total). No credit card required. If 30 days isn’t enough for our catalog size, research suggests MRPeasy commonly grants extensions for complex setups — worth asking on day 1.

Answered — Contract & Lock-In

Month-to-month, no contract, no hidden fees. Cancel anytime. Annual billing saves ~8% (1 month free). No mandatory support contract — dropping it does NOT disable integrations (unlike Fishbowl).
